Privacy Policy

Transparency is important to us. Here is how we handle your data.

Last Updated: January 29, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to OmixCalculator. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you may provide us through the Website. This Privacy Policy explains what information may be collected on our Website, how we use this information, and under what circumstances we may disclose the information to third parties.

2. How We Process Calculations (Client-Side)

Your data stays on your device. Unlike many other online tools, OmixCalculator is built using "Client-Side" technology (JavaScript). This means:

  • When you enter numbers, grades, or personal metrics (like weight or age) into our calculators, the calculation is performed locally by your own web browser.
  • Your input data is never transmitted to our servers or stored in any database.
  • Once you close the browser tab, your input data is completely erased.

3. Information We Collect

Since we do not require user accounts, we collect very little data. However, we may collect:

  • Contact Information: If you voluntarily reach out to us via our Contact Form, we will receive your name and email address. We use this solely to reply to your inquiry.
  • Usage Data: We may use standard web analytics tools (like Google Analytics) to understand how visitors interact with our site (e.g., which pages are most popular). This data is anonymized and cannot identify you personally.

  • Opt-Out: Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings.

5. Third-Party Links

Our Service may contain links to external sites that are not operated by us. If you click on a third-party link, you will be directed to that third party's site. We strongly advise you to review the Privacy Policy and terms of every site you visit.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this article.
