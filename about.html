Our Story

The Omix Mission

Digital tools often come with a hidden cost: intrusive ads, slow loading times, or forced registrations. We built OmixCalculator to change that. We wanted a place where a student could calculate their CGPA or a developer could generate a secure password without the clutter.

Today, our suite of tools serves thousands of users daily, ranging from financial planning and health monitoring to academic grading and software development utilities. We believe in the "Unix Philosophy" applied to the web: build tools that do one thing, and do it extremely well.
